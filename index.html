Wedding warm-up

Ceremony rehearsal

Central Park, The Cop Cot, Friday 2pm to 3pm

For those involved in the ceremony procession (you know who you are) we will have a practice run-through at the arbour in Central Park.

Rehearsal picnic

Prospect Park, Friday 4pm to 7pm

To those coming to the ceremony, join us for a relaxed picnic in Prospect Park on the day before the wedding. Please bring a dish or something to "throw on the grill" (as Lauren puts it), and whatever you would like to drink. (To all you free and easy Europeans, alcohol must be well-concealed.)

Wedding day

Ceremony

Central Park, Saturday 4pm

We'll have an intimate ceremony with our close friends and family at the Cop Cot arbour in Central Park at 4pm. Be prepared to be outside in the warm air for a thirty minute ceremony.

Reception

Manny Cantor Center, Saturday 6pm to 10pm

We'll have a super fun party at the Manny Cantor Center from 6pm to 10pm. There will be a simple summer supper, with gluten free and vegan options. There will be intoxicating drinks and dancing. The dress code: easy-going, but snappy.


Wedding warm-down

Family brunch

We'd love for our now-joined families to come over to help us eat the left-overs from the night before. Feel free to bring luggage if you are on your way to the airport.
